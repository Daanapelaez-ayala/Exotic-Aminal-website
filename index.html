Exotic Animals: Axolotl

What Are Axolotls?

- The Axolotl, Ambystoma mexicanum, is a type of salamander that doesn't go through metamorphosis.

Fun Facts

1. axolotls can regrow their own body parts

2. Female axolotls lay up to 1,000 eggs at a time.

3. axolotls are now critically endangered.
